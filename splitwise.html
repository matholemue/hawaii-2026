💰 Splitwise

Expense tracking for the trip

How It Works

Mom & Dad are generously covering the trip. Splitwise lets each of us log what we spend — dinners, groceries, activities, Ubers — and at the end, Mom & Dad reimburse everyone in one go. No chasing each other for $20 here and there.

Setup (2 minutes)

  1. Click the link above — it takes you to the Splitwise website
  2. Sign up — use your Google account or email, completely free
  3. You'll see Hawaii Labor Day 2026 — tap Join
  4. Download the Splitwise app on your phone (optional but way easier during the trip)

During the Trip

Adding an expense

Tap + → Enter amount → Split equally → Add a note → Done

Example

"$120 — dinner at Duke's" → split equally with everyone. The app tracks it. That's it.

At the end

Mom & Dad see the full tally and reimburse everyone. No spreadsheets, no math, no awkward Venmo requests.
