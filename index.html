Imara Guidelines

A few things to keep our 1-year-old safe and her parents sane. Guardrails, not walls.

1
Water = Mom & Dad only. Pool, ocean, splash pad — only Sujita or Matthew handle Imara in or near water. Not about trust; parental instinct.
2
Ask first. Want to take her somewhere, feed her something, pick her up? Quick "can I?" goes a long way.
3
Nap schedule is sacred. We'll share her rhythm. Please don't wake a sleeping baby — even for a photo.
4
Sun protection on us. We handle sunscreen, hat, shade. If her hat flies off, tell us — we'll grab it.
5
Let her come to you. She warms up at her own pace. No grab-and-go.
