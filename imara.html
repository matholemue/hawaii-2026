👶 Imara Guidelines

Keeping our girl safe and her parents sane

Imara will be about 15 months old during the trip. She's mobile, curious, and has opinions. These aren't walls — they're guardrails so everyone can relax and enjoy the trip.

1

Water = Mom & Dad only

Pool, ocean, splash pad — only Sujita or Matthew handle Imara in or near water. This isn't about trust; it's parental instinct. You're all welcome to splash with us, though.

2

Ask first

Want to take her somewhere, feed her something new, pick her up? A quick "can I?" goes a long way. She knows what she wants — and what she doesn't.

3

Nap schedule is sacred

We'll share her rough rhythm. Overtired baby = nobody's vacation. Please don't wake a sleeping Imara — even for an adorable photo.

4

Sun protection on us

We handle sunscreen, hat, shade. If her hat flies off or she looks pink, tell us — we'll fix it. You don't need to be the sunscreen police.

5

Let her come to you

She'll warm up to everyone at her own pace. No grab-and-go — and that's totally fine. By day three she'll be your best friend.

Bottom line: Relaxed Sujita = relaxed trip = everyone wins.
