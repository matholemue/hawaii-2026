🤿 Activities

Ideas for Maui — add yours

On the List

🤿

Scuba Diving

Shore diving at Makena Landing and Honolua Bay — no boat needed. Turtles, reef fish, beginner-friendly.

Michael
⛵

Beach Catamaran

Rent a small catamaran — take turns going out. Easy to learn, no license needed for small boats.

Michael
🛣️

Road to Hana

Full day trip. Waterfalls, black sand beach at Waiʻānapanapa, banana bread stands, 600+ curves. Leave early.

🌄

Haleakalā Sunrise

10,000 ft summit. Dress warm — it's ~40°F at the top. Reservations required 60 days in advance.

Also Worth Considering

Snorkeling

Honolua Bay, Kapalua Bay, Black Rock. Check daily snorkel report each morning.

Lūʻau

Old Lāhainā Lūʻau is the classic. Book well ahead — sells out weeks in advance.

Surf Lessons

Lāhainā has gentle breaks for beginners. Group lessons are ~$80/person.

ʻĪao Valley

Easy paved hike, lush tropical valley, iconic 1,200-ft ʻĪao Needle peak. Good for all ages — stroller-friendly path.

Whale Watching

Peak season is Dec–May, so we're too early. But keep it on the list for next time — Maui is whale central.

Beach Day

Kāʻanapali Beach right at the resort. Walk the boardwalk to Whalers Village for shopping, shave ice, and sunset cocktails at Duke's.
